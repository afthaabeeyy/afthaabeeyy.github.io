മാറ്റം
(The Exchange)


Genre: Visual / Symbolic / Anti-Drug
Duration: ~5 Minutes
Dialogues: None (only Narration + Music)
Language: Malayalam

Main Cast:
Aarav – A young man (18–20)
Shopkeeper – Mysterious, expressionless

Optional extras: Background silhouettes or shadow people
---



SCENE 1: തെരുവും കടയും (The Street and the Shop)

Time: 0:00 – 1:00
Location: Dimly lit street, evening after rain.

SHOT 1 – Wide shot – Aarav walking alone under a flickering streetlight. Raindrops on the road reflect light. Lighting: Cold blue tone.
Sound: Slow piano + light rain + faint heartbeat.

SHOT 2 – Tracking shot (behind Aarav) – Camera follows his steps.

SHOT 3 – Aarav stops, looks to his right — a faint golden glow appears from an unknown alley.

SHOT 4 – Close-up of Aarav’s eyes widening with curiosity.

SHOT 5 – Medium shot – He walks toward the glow and finds a small antique shop. A glowing signboard reads:
> “ദുഃഖം വിട്ട് സന്തോഷം നേടാം.”

SHOT 6 – Over-the-shoulder shot – Aarav pushes the door open; a soft bell sound.

NARRATION (soft voice):
> “ആശയുടെ വേഷം ധരിച്ച ഇരുട്ടിലേക്കാണ് അവൻ കയറിയത്…” ---



SCENE 2: ആദ്യ ഇടപാട് (The First Trade)

Time: 1:00 – 2:00
Location: Inside the shop — dim golden light, smoky atmosphere.

SHOT 7 – Wide shot – Shelves filled with glowing bottles labeled ‘ആത്മവിശ്വാസം’, ‘സന്തോഷം’, ‘ശാന്തി’.

SHOT 8 – Close-up of Aarav’s hand touching a bottle.

SHOT 9 – Insert shot – Label reads “ആത്മവിശ്വാസം.”

SHOT 10 – Medium shot – Shopkeeper slides the bottle toward him. No eye contact, slow movement.

SHOT 11 – Close-up – Aarav hesitates, then places a few coins on the counter (they shimmer faintly).
Lighting: Golden at first, turns slightly dim when he drinks the potion.

SHOT 12 – Extreme close-up – Aarav’s eyes brighten for a second, then fade.
NARRATION: > “ഓരോ സന്തോഷത്തിനും വിലയുണ്ട്… കാണാൻ കഴിയാത്തതെങ്കിലും തീർച്ചയായുണ്ട്.”

---


SCENE 3: വില കൂടുന്നു (The Cost Increases)

Time: 2:00 – 3:30

SHOT 13 – Montage sequence:
Aarav returning to the shop again and again. Each time, he looks weaker. Shopkeeper demands different things: a photo, bracelet, book. Aarav gives them one by one.
Lighting: Each visit → darker tones, more shadows on Aarav’s face. Music: Slightly distorted violin + heartbeat intensifies.

SHOT 14 – Close-up – Aarav’s trembling hand giving away a family photo.

SHOT 15 – Low angle shot – Shopkeeper’s expression remains blank, symbolic of addiction’s control.

NARRATION: > “താൻ വിറ്റുകൊടുക്കുന്നത് എത്രയെന്നു പോലും അവൻ തിരിച്ചറിയാതെ പോയി…”

---


SCENE 4: തിരിച്ചറിവ് (The Realization)

Time: 3:30 – 4:30

SHOT 16 – Dark interior room. Bottles scattered everywhere, glowing faintly. Aarav kneels in the center.

SHOT 17 – Close-up – His reflection in a broken mirror — pale face, empty eyes.

SHOT 18 – Insert – Bottles flicker and lose light one by one.

SHOT 19 – Slow motion – Aarav drops a bottle. It shatters.
Sound: Glass breaking echoes → fades into silence.
Lighting: Only a single white beam from the window.

NARRATION: > “മയക്കുമരുന്ന് തരുന്നത് ഒന്നുമല്ല… അത് മോഷ്ടിക്കുന്നത് മാത്രം.”

---

SCENE 5: വെളിച്ചത്തിലേക്ക് (Towards the Light)

Time: 4:30 – 5:00

SHOT 20 – Wide shot – Morning sunlight through trees. Aarav slowly walks out of darkness into bright light.

SHOT 21 – Close-up – His face calm, eyes closed, breathing deep.
Sound: Hopeful music (piano + soft strings).

SHOT 22 – Camera tilts up toward the sky — bright sunlight fills the frame.

NARRATION: > “ഒരു വിനിമയം, എല്ലാം നഷ്ടമാക്കും. അതിൽ നിന്നും അകന്നു നിൽക്കൂ…”

ON-SCREEN TEXT: > “മാറ്റം വേണ്ട — മയക്കുമരുന്ന് വേണ്ട.” (Say NO to Drugs)

Music fades out slowly. Screen to black.
